Submit Your Antarctica Cruise Operator for Review

We independently review and rank expedition cruise operators based on IAATO compliance, fleet specifications, and verified traveler feedback. Operators meeting our criteria are considered for inclusion in future updates.

Eligibility Criteria

✅

To be considered for review, operators must meet all of the following:

  • ✓ Active IAATO membership (verifiable at iaato.org)
  • ✓ Minimum 2 completed Antarctic seasons
  • ✓ Verified vessel specifications and passenger capacity
  • ✓ Publicly listed departure dates and pricing

What Happens Next

We review all submissions within 30 days. Meeting eligibility criteria does not guarantee inclusion. Our rankings are determined solely by our editorial team based on objective criteria. No operator pays for placement or ranking position.

If we require additional information, we will contact you at the email address provided. We do not notify operators when a submission does not meet our criteria.
