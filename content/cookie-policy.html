Cookie Policy

Antarctica Cruise Comparison uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. This policy explains what we use and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember your preferences and usage patterns. Some cookies are essential for site functionality; others are used for analytics or advertising.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

We do not use any essential session cookies of our own. The site functions fully without any cookies.

Third-Party Cookies: Google Maps API

Our review cards use the Google Places API to retrieve up-to-date operator ratings and reviews. When this API loads, Google may set cookies on your device subject to Google's Privacy Policy. These cookies may include identifiers used by Google to measure API usage. We do not control these cookies and do not have access to their data.

If the Google Places API is unavailable or blocked, all review card content falls back to hardcoded static data — the page functions identically without the API.

Analytics

We currently do not use analytics cookies or tracking software on this site.

Advertising

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or retargeting technologies of any kind.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the core functionality of this site. To manage Google's cookies specifically, visit Google's privacy controls.

Changes to This Policy

If we add new functionality that changes our cookie usage, we will update this policy. Last updated: June 2026.
